NAME OF PROGRAM: Computer Engineering

ENTRANCE REQUIREMENTS
General admission requirements:
Final grade of 70% in Grade 11 or Grade 12 English
(or equivalent course, including provincial examinations where applicable).
Graduation from high school with a minimum average of 70% (or higher for many programs).

Degree-specific requirements: Applied Science (Engineering)
Grade 12 - ENG4U, MHF4U, SCH4U, SPH4U, Two other Grade 12 4U or 4M courses
Grade 11 - Chemistry 11 (SCH3U), Physics 11 (SPH3U)

LENGTH OF PROGRAM: Four years
